BMW Mobile Service

White BMW parked. Our BMW Mobile Service Vehicle Will Come Right to Your Driveway or Workplace!

If you’re in the market for convenient and affordable auto service, our Habberstad BMW Mobile Service Vehicle is equipped to come to your driveway or workplace and fix your vehicle on the spot. Instead of finding time during your busy workday to drop off your vehicle in Huntington Station, we’ll come directly to you and perform most services and repairs!

Mobile BMW Services

No matter where you are, Habberstad BMW of Huntington Mobile Service Van will come to you to perform most routine and advanced automotive repairs. Our team is professionally trained to administer mobile assistance and service in the field when you need it most, including recall repairs for replacing driver airbags, PCV valves, and blower motor harnesses.

But that’s not all! In addition to replacing old or worn out parts, our BMW Mobile Service Vehicle is also equipped to help you manage other convenient services, including:

  • Oil, Coolant & Fluid Top Offs
  • Oil and Filter Changes
  • Spare Tire Replacement
  • Vehicle Jump Starts
  • Fuel Replenishment – Emergency fills (2 gallons) to get to a gas station
  • Battery Replacement
  • And more!
