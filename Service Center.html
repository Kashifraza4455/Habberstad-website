Having aligned wheels and tires is the most important step to balance the wear and performance of the tires on your BMW. This should be part of your normal maintenance routine for your BMW and we are happy to provide the service at Habberstad BMW of Huntingon! Driving while aligned wheels on your BMW can help to ensure the safety of your Ultimate Driving Machine. This also helps save you money in the long run by extending the life expectancy of your tires allowing you to not replace them as often.

Why Is It Important to Align My BMW Wheels?

When your BMW has wheels and tires that are not aligned, driving on them will make your tires lose tread more quickly. Not only is this dangerous, but it is also the number one reason customers have to replace their tires. Failing to align your tires does not lead to an enjoyable driving experience.

Why Do Wheels Come Out of Alignment?

Simply putting new tires on your BMW can alter the wheel alignment of your vehicle. Other driving mishaps such as hitting a pothole, running over a curb, or getting into an unfortunate accident can cause your wheels to be misaligned. Habberstad BMW of Huntington offers wheel alignment with tire purchase as well as throughout the rest of your BMW ownership by scheduling an appointment with our service department.

How Do I Know When I Should Have My BMW Wheel Alignment Checked?

It is important to have your Ultimate Driving Machine serviced by Habberstad BMW of Huntington any time you have new tires installed so we can make sure they are aligned. If you notice your tires are not wearing uniformly, it would also be a great time to bring your SUV or car into our Service Department for an alignment appointment. Lastly, you should also get the alignment checked after you hit something hard or get into even a minor car accident.

Having your wheels properly aligned varies in cost depending on the BMW you drive. The longer you drive around the Long Island area without proper tire alignment, the more costly it could be to fix.

All new BMW vehicles come equipped with the BMW tire pressure monitoring system. The system tracks the tire pressure of each tire on your vehicle, displaying the result in either your Control Display or instrument panel. When a tire is over or underinflated, you will know about it immediately, so you can take appropriate action. The Tire Pressure Monitoring system will display a warning signal and indicate which tire(s) need attention and will alert you via the Control Display or multifunctional instrument display.

Tire Pressure Monitoring Extends the Life of Your BMW Tires

When you drive a BMW with a tire pressure monitoring system, your vehicle will require less tire-related assistance from our Service Center. Maintaining the appropriate tire pressure reduces wear and tear damage, which assists in extending the life of your tires.

Improved Handling and Safety

Maintaining the right tire pressure improves the handling of your BMW as it allows your tires to maintain the optimal contact with the road, increasing your ability to safely maneuver around corners, objects, and apply your brakes rapidly if necessary.
